— Our Story —

Born in the
Dark Hours

"Great coffee is not brewed.
It is composed."
— Arjun Rajan, Founder

NOIR was conceived at 2am in a small apartment in Chennai, when our founder Arjun Rajan realized that the city he loved deserved a café that matched its hidden sophistication. A place that understood silence, depth, and the quiet luxury of a perfectly made cup.

We source our beans directly from estates in Coorg, Araku Valley, and Yirgacheffe, Ethiopia — working with farmers who share our obsession with quality. Every roast batch is profiled by hand. Every espresso is pulled with intention.

Our kitchen follows the same philosophy: no shortcuts, no compromise. Ingredients are sourced daily. Bread is baked at dawn. The menu changes with the seasons.

01

Obsessive Quality

Every element of every dish and drink is scrutinized, refined, and elevated before it reaches you.

02

Direct Sourcing

We know the names of the farmers who grow our coffee. That relationship is the foundation of our flavour.

03

Seasonal & Local

Our menu is a living document — rewritten each season to honour what's freshest and most beautiful.
